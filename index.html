FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to join?

Not at all. Most of our adult classes are designed for complete beginners, and our Kids class starts from the very basics too.

What should I wear to class?

Comfortable, fitted clothing you can move in. Ballet shoes are recommended but not required for your first class.

What age can my child start?

Our Kids class welcomes dancers from age 3. From there, students move through Junior A, Junior B, and Teen as they grow.

Can I try a class before signing up?

Yes — just reach out through our inquiry form and we'll help you find the right class to start with.
