About

Light as a feather.
Welcoming at every step.

Since opening in 2014, Plume Ballet Studio has grown together with our community. From our littlest beginners to adults discovering ballet for the first time, we welcome every age and every level of experience — and we celebrate every small "I did it!" along the way.
